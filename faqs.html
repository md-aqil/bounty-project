We are here to help you

  • What is a bounty campaign?

    A bounty campaign is a new model of product promotion by the community of influencers. It can also be seen as word of mouth campaign of digital media.

  • What is a bounty campaign?

    You can participate in any campaign listed on our website. You can choose the tasks in which you are comfortable and earn rewards after successful completion of the task.

  • How and when will I get my rewards?

    As a reward for your tasks done for a project, you earn the coins/token of that project after the end of the ICO. The exact date is announced by the project at the time of ICO.

  • How much does it cost to join as Influencer?

    It's free to join any task listed on our platform. Our aim is to help you earn cryptocurrency.

  • What is the value of the coins/tokens received?

    The value of the rewards is not set by any particular group. At the time of ICO, the price is set for token sale by the project. After the ICO, when the coin/token is listed on an exchange, the price is determined by the public.

  • Where will I get my rewards?

    In your profile section, you are asked to save your Ethereum address from a native wallet. Most of the ICOs have ERC20 token as a reward. So you will recieve those tokens in your saved Ethereum address.

  • How to list a project on this website?

    If you are a project who wants to run bounty campaign with us, just apply as Brand and fill the form. We will reach out to you shortly.
